What you'll need at your interview

When you come to your interview, make sure you have access to your GOV.UK Verify identity account. You'll need your

  • Email address or username
  • Password
  • Mobile phone registered to the account

At your interview, the hiring manager will take a photo of you. The photo will be automatically checked against the photo of you held on file by Her Majesty's Passport Office (HMPO).

You don't need to bring your passport to your interview.

You might also need other things for your interview that your hiring manager will tell you about.
